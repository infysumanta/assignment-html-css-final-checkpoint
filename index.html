About Provaswito


Provaswito started its journey in 2011 with some of the young, eloquent, aspired artists and they started organizing yearlys contexting painting, sculpture and photography, every year in occasion of basant Panchami or Saraswati Puja. It germinated in the enriched soil of Hetampur, Birbhum (land of pride), WB. Many artists from the different countries across the world register their names for participation and they also encourage and appreciate such efforts. It is still growing year after year with the efforts and inclinations towards its betterment and radiates as todayâ€™s Provaswito, a spirited centre for cultivating fine arts and an organization of young, devoted, dedicated, determined artists, cordially nurturing art and culture.
